## Notes

**Personalization Variables:**
Use these in Brevo templates:
- {{ contact.FIRSTNAME }} - First name
- {{ contact.LASTNAME }} - Last name
- {{ contact.EMAIL }} - Email address
- {{ contact.PHONE }} - Phone number

**Links to Update:**
Before sending emails, update these placeholders:
- [ZOOM_LINK] - Your Zoom meeting link
- [MEETING_ID] - Zoom meeting ID
- [PASSCODE] - Zoom passcode
- [RECORDING_LINK_DAY1] - Day 1 recording (after workshop)
- [RECORDING_LINK_DAY2] - Day 2 recording (after workshop)
- [FEEDBACK_FORM_LINK] - Google Form for feedback
- [LINKEDIN_PROFILE] - Your LinkedIn URL
- [YOUTUBE_CHANNEL] - Your YouTube channel URL
- [YOUR_PHONE_NUMBER] - Your WhatsApp/phone number

**Best Practices:**
- Send confirmation email immediately (within 1 minute of registration)
- Send morning reminders exactly 2 hours before workshop
- Always include Zoom link in every reminder
- Personalize wherever possible
- Keep emails concise but informative
- Always include an easy way to reply/contact you
- Test all links before sending to full list
