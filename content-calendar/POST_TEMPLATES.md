### 3. Personal Story (Story Format)

```

[The struggle/challenge - what went wrong - 2-3 lines]

[The turning point - what changed - 2-3 lines]

[The lesson - what you learned - 2-3 lines]

[Takeaway for the reader - make it about them]

[Optional: Question to drive engagement]

---
#CareerJourney #TechLife #LessonsLearned #SoftwareEngineering #Growth
```

**Example:**
```
I mass-applied to 200+ companies in 3 months.

Got 5 callbacks. 2 interviews. 0 offers.

I was doing everything "right" - updating resume daily, applying everywhere, practicing LeetCode.

But I was missing one thing: I wasn't learning from rejections.

Then I started tracking:
- Which companies responded
- What questions I struggled with
- Where I lost confidence

Pattern emerged. Fixed it. Got 3 offers in the next month.

Rejection isn't failure. It's feedback.

What's one rejection that taught you something valuable?

---
#CareerJourney #TechLife #LessonsLearned #JobSearch #InterviewPrep
```
